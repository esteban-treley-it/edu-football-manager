## 1. High Concept
EduFootball Manager is a PC football management simulation built in Unreal Engine 5.5 (C++) that teaches:
- Tactical football understanding
- Data analysis
- Financial planning
- Psychology and leadership
- Systems thinking

All learning is delivered in two layers:
- **Hidden education** through natural gameplay.
- **Explicit education** through Academy & School Mode.

The game targets:
- Football fans
- Students of sports science
- Aspiring coaches
- Strategy and management players

---

## 2. Core Pillars

1. Tactical Depth
- Real formations and positional play
- Player roles with real behavioral impact
- Game phases: build-up, progression, final third, pressing, transitions

2. Systems Thinking
- Every decision has multi-system consequences
- Training affects injuries, morale, finances, results

3. Education Through Play
- Players learn football concepts implicitly
- Explicit learning only appears in Academy Mode

4. Long-Term Simulation
- Multi-season careers
- Player aging, decline, and development
- Club financial cycles and risk

5. Adaptive Challenge
- AI adapts to player behavior
- Forces evolution, not repetition

---

## 3. Game Modes
